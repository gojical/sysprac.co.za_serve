SANS 1393 - Construction management Systems

In a competitive environment that is constantly becoming more discerning, cost conscious, and sensitive to health, safety and environmental issues, SysPrac is proud to offer the Construction Management System (CMS) a South African Standard (SANS 1393).

This unique and hybrid standard, specifically developed for the construction industry incorporates a triad of international standards, specifically, quality, environmental and health and safety management with a pragmatic and explicit focus on the effective and efficient use of construction resources.

Technically this product offers the solution to South African Construction companies that are required to work within the minefield of legislation and industry standards such as SANS 10400 (Building standards), the Occupational Health and safety Act, and various national, provincial and regional environmental laws, to name but a few.
