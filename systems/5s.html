5S - Housekeeping and Cleanliness

Irrespective of the size, nature and complexity of the manufacturing process, the chaotic world of production is made so much easier through the implementation and correct use of the 5’s program. The fundamentals of functional discipline create the all-important culture changing initiative, which encourages workplace cleanliness and orderliness, the 5’s program is a methodical and sustainable instrument that enhances productivity.

5’s is an acronym for the five stages required to catapult an organisation from ordinary to extraordinary.
